Lockjaw

Lockjaw can actually teleport himslef, and those touching him, to the moon and back!!!

  • Species: Inhuman bulldog
  • Origin: Attilan (moon)
  • Size: Gigantic
  • Abilities: Super-canine strength in his jaw, Teleportation, Precognition, Matter ingestion

Lockjaw is a fictional, gigantic, bulldog-like being appearing in several comic books created by Marvel comics. Lockjaw has some cool super powers, the most useful of them is the ability to teleport. Recently he appeared in a tv show called Marvel's inhumans. In contrast with the rest of the show, he managed to impress us with his literally immense cuteness!
